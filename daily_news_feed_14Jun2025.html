Image not found.Pension Account Attached for Income Tax Dues: Madras HC Allows Withdrawal Citing S.11 of TN Pension Act - Taxscan

Published Date: Fri, 13 Jun 2025

The Madras High Court ruled in favor of S. Devarajan, allowing him to withdraw ₹48,000 monthly from his pension account, which was previously attached for tax recovery. Devarajan had filed a writ petition against DBS Bank, seeking the lifting of the attachment. The Department of Revenue initiated recovery proceedings due to a tax liability of ₹1 crore, stemming from an assessment order that treated transactions in Devarajan's wife's joint account as unexplained income. However, the court considered Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Pension Act, which protects pensions from attachment, and allowed the withdrawal based on Devarajan's age, the transaction's date (2013), and his pending appeal. The court also directed the appellate authority to expedite the appeal process.
