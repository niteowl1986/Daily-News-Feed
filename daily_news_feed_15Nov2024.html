Image not found.DTB, Airtel Money, and Credable Launch “Kwasa Kwasa” to Advance Financial Inclusion - Nile Post

Published Date: Thu, 14 Nov 2024

Kwasa Kwasa is a mobile banking service that provides quick and affordable short-term loans to Airtel Money customers in Uganda. The service is a partnership between Diamond Trust Bank, Airtel Money, and Credable Group, aimed at closing financial access gaps in rural and underserved areas. Kwasa Kwasa offers loans starting at UGX 5,000 without collateral, allowing users to borrow and repay amounts easily and affordably. The service is accessible via USSD code 18583# or the My Airtel App, with a 30-day repayment period and the option to roll over. The CEO of Diamond Trust Bank, Godfrey Sebaana, and the Managing Director of Airtel Mobile Commerce Uganda, Japhet Aritho, expressed excitement about the potential of Kwasa Kwasa to advance financial inclusion in Uganda. The service aims to empower more Ugandans to manage their finances effectively, contributing to the country's vision for inclusive economic growth and sustainable development.

Grasim's Q2 profit falls 46 pc to Rs 1,100 cr, dragged by UltraTech and higher interest depreciation - Deccan Herald

Published Date: Thu, 14 Nov 2024

Grasim Industries Ltd, a flagship holding firm of the Aditya Birla Group, reported a 14% decline in consolidated net profit to Rs 1, crore in Q2 FY25. The decline was attributed to lower profitability in the cement business and higher interest and depreciation charges. Revenue from operations grew 10% to Rs 33, crore, driven by the performance of Financial Services, Cellulosic Staple Fibre, and Specialty Chemicals businesses. The company's cement business, UltraTech, reported a 4% YoY growth in sales volumes, but faced lower realisations. Grasim's new paints business, Birla Opus, is ramping up production, and its B2B ecommerce business, Birla Pivot, is on track to achieve revenue of USD 1 billion in three years. The company's outlook is positive, with its diverse presence across businesses positioning it to capitalize on opportunities in India's fast-growing economy.
