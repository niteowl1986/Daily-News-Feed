Image not found.Health insurance premiums rising nearly €10 per month in 2025 - NL Times

Published Date: Wed, 13 Nov 2024

Four major health insurers in the Netherlands, Zilveren Kruis, VGZ, Menzis, and CZ, are increasing their monthly premiums by an average of 10-14 euros next year. The insurers blame the price hikes on rising healthcare costs, inflation, and an aging population. The premium increases will affect 85% of policyholders in the Netherlands. Zilveren Kruis CEO Georgette Fijneman acknowledged that the increase is steep, but noted that the healthcare allowance will cover part of the premium for low-income households. Menzis is advocating for the healthcare allowance to be paid directly to health insurers, rather than to individuals, to ensure the funds are used for healthcare. Menzis and Zilveren Kruis also expressed concerns that halving the healthcare deductible, as planned by the government, will lead to higher premiums and increased demand for care, resulting in a shortage of 20,000 workers in healthcare.

Kumar Mangalam Birla's Aditya Birla Group may benefit from US President-elect Donald Trump's "America First" policies.
