IDFC First Bank credit card changes from February 2025: All you need to know - The Economic Times

Published Date: Wed, 12 Feb 2025

IDFC FIRST Bank has revised the terms and features of its credit card. Changes include revision of the Annual Percentage Rate (APR), fuel fee & surcharge waiver, statement & payment due date, education fee payments, railway lounge access, addon card fee, card replacement fee, and more. Statement date for FIRST Millennia, FIRST Wealth, and FIRST SWYP Credit Cards will be 20th of each month. Payments for education fees done using thirdparty platforms like CRED, PayTM, Cheq, MobiKwik, and others will be subject to a 1% fee.

Image not found.DBS Bank India opens flagship branch in Coimbatore, expanding presence in Tamil Nadu - Mediabrief

Published Date: Wed, 12 Feb 2025

DBS Bank India has opened a new premium branch in Coimbatore, targeting a diverse customer base. The branch will cater to high-net-worth individuals (HNIs) and non-resident Indians (NRIs) through its DBS Treasures proposition, offering bespoke benefits and cross-border solutions. Small and medium enterprises (SMEs) will benefit from a comprehensive portfolio of financial services, including trade and FX solutions, and working capital facilities, leveraging DBS's extensive Asian network. Recognizing Coimbatore's growing real estate market, the bank will offer Consumer Loan Against Property (CLAP) to meet the financing needs of small businesses. DBS Golden Circle, a customized proposition for retirees and pensioners, will provide higher deposit rates, discounts, healthcare benefits, and priority services. With a focus on a seamless phygital experience, DBS aims to become the bank of choice for a wide range of clients in Coimbatore.
