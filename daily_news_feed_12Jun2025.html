Image not found.Consumer Court Raps Tata AIG - Deccan Chronicle

Published Date: Wed, 11 Jun 2025

The Telangana State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission has ruled in favor of PNB Housing Finance Limited in a housing loan insurance claim dispute with Tata AIG Insurance Company. The case involved the death of the loan holder, Suddala Gowd Suddula, who had a policy with Tata AIG covering his loan of Rs crore. Tata AIG initially rejected the claim, citing illness-related death as not covered under the policy. However, the Commission upheld the earlier ruling of the Hyderabad District Consumer Commission, finding that the policy definition of "Accident" encompassed the circumstances of Suddula's death. The Commission ordered Tata AIG to pay Rs crore to PNB Housing Finance and Rs 1 lakh in compensation to the deceased's wife, Suddala Sujatha. This decision highlights the importance of carefully reviewing insurance policy terms and the consumer protection mechanisms available in India.
