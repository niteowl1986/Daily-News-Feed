Bajaj Allianz Life Unveils Nifty 500 Multicap Momentum Quality 50 Fund - SMEStreet

Published Date: Wed, 05 Feb 2025

Bajaj Allianz Life Insurance, one of India's leading private life insurer, today announced the launch of its new index based fund. The fund aims to replicate the performance of the benchmark index (Nifty 500 Multicap Momentum Quality 50 Index), subject to tracking error. With a multicap approach and historical dynamic allocation among large Cap, Mid Cap & Small Cap, the fund identifies opportunities across market segments. It offers a path to longterm capital appreciation while navigating market fluctuations.
