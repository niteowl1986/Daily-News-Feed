Czech-Slovak relations chill to glacial - DW

Published Date: Sun, 01 Jun 2025

Czechia and Slovakia have been independent states for over 30 years. Until recently, the bond between them was extremely close. But the subject of Ukraine, among other things, has driven a wedge between the neighbors. Relations between the Czech Republic and Slovakia since the peaceful dissolution of Czechoslovakia in 1992 have been consistently good, close and characterized by mutual trust. The governments of the two neighboring countries have supported each other and cooperated on defense. The Slovak opposition, which is led by the Progressive Slovakia (PS) party, has good relations with Prague.

UGRO Capital launches ₹400-crore rights issue to power MSME lending growth - Fortune India

Published Date: Sun, 01 Jun 2025

UGRO Capital, a DataTechdriven NBFC specializing in MSME financing, has launched a 400 crore rights issue, pricing the shares at 162 apiece. The move follows a recent 915 crore preferential issue via compulsorily convertible debentures (CCDs), as the company seeks to scale up its asset book and expand its national footprint. The rights issue has already attracted commitments of over 250 crore, including 150 crore from IFU, an affiliate of the Danish government.
