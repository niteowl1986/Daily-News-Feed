Bajaj Allianz Life Goes Live With Bima-ASBA Facility, The New UPI-Enabled Payment Mechanism For Policy - News18

Published Date: Sun, 23 Feb 2025

Bajaj Allianz Life has become the first insurer to go live with the BIMA Application Supported by Blocked Amount facility (BimaASBA) Under this IRDAI initiative, policyholders can opt for UPI s OneTime Mandate (OTM) and authorise the blocking of a specified amount. The amount will be debited only after the insurance company completes the underwriting process and decides to accept the proposal.

Image not found.Bajaj Allianz becomes first insurer offering Bima-ASBA: What is it? - NewsBytes

Published Date: Sun, 23 Feb 2025

Bajaj Allianz Life has become the first company to introduce the BimaASBA facility. Facility enables policyholders to block a certain amount (of up to 2 lakh) in their bank account via UPI's OneTime Mandate (OTM) The amount is deducted only after the insurer has underwritten and approved the policy proposal. IRDAI has mandated all insurers to provide the feature by March 1, 2025.
