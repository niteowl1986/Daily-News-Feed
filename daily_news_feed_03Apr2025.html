Published Date: Wed, 02 Apr 2025

Tepid income growth, higher operating expenses and impairment charges saw Rabobank NZ record a % dip in its net profit after tax for the 2024 year. Despite strong growth in our lending portfolio, our income was largely unchanged as a result of strong pricing competition across the agri lending market.
