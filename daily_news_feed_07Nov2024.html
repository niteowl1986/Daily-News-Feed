Image not found.IDFC FIRST Bank Launches Real-Time International Money Transfer Tracking - Adda247

Published Date: Wed, 06 Nov 2024

IDFC FIRST Bank has launched India's first real-time tracking for international money transfers in partnership with Swift. The service allows customers to track cross-border transactions with complete transparency, providing updates on the status of funds. The service is available on the bank's mobile app and internet banking platforms, aligning with its "Customer First" philosophy. IDFC FIRST Bank is the first Indian bank to offer end-to-end traceability for cross-border payments, providing customers with real-time updates on the transfer process. The Swift GPI integration enables users to monitor international transfers in real-time, alerting them to potential issues and allowing them to take corrective action. The bank is offering free real-time tracking under the "Pay Abroad" feature, which is particularly beneficial for purposes permitted under the Reserve Bank of India's Liberalised Remittance Scheme.

DBS Private Bank Lifts Lid On Indian HNW Diaspora, Family Office Growth - WealthBriefingAsia

Published Date: Wed, 06 Nov 2024

DBS Private Bank has released a white paper on the Indian family office industry, which has seen a surge in growth in recent years. The report highlights the importance of the sector as an opportunity for growth, particularly in Singapore and other major financial hubs. The number of Indian family offices has increased from 45 in 2018 to around 300 today, with many families setting up offices in Singapore, Hong Kong, and Dubai. The report also notes that the Indian diaspora is growing its wealth, with many establishing family offices in jurisdictions worldwide. DBS Private Bank is well-positioned to capitalize on this trend, with its DBS Multi Family Office Foundry VCC, which uses Singapore's Variable Capital Company structure. The report provides insights from 13 major entrepreneurs and philanthropists from the Indian diaspora, highlighting the importance of family offices in Asia and the potential for growth in the region.

IFC Participates in Bajaj Finance’s USD 1 Billion Fund Raise to Help Boost Climate Finance, Empower Women in India - IFC Press Releases

Published Date: Wed, 06 Nov 2024

IFC is the largest global development institution focused on the private sector in developing countries. It aims to promote development by providing debt and equity to the private sector through various products. IFC has partnered with Bajaj Finance Limited to invest $400 million to improve access to climate finance for electric vehicles and energy-efficient consumer goods in India. The partnership will also support women-owned microenterprises and women microborrowers.
