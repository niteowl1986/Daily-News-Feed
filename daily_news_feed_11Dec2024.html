Image not found.FICO Launches Groundbreaking Credit Program to Reach 53M Underserved Americans - StockTitan

Published Date: Tue, 10 Dec 2024

FICO has launched the Lenders Leading Inclusion Program to help Community Development Financial Institutions (CDFIs) and Minority Depository Institutions (MDIs) expand credit access for underserved communities. The program provides access to FICO Score XD and UltraFICO Score, which can reach over 50% of the US credit-eligible population. The initiative addresses the needs of nearly 53 million US consumers with limited or no credit bureau data, including 25 million "credit invisible" individuals. Participating institutions will receive alternative data scoring tools, program adoption resources, and inclusion-led lending strategy consulting. The program aims to responsibly expand credit access to more people and help consumers achieve financial opportunities. The initiative positions FICO strategically in the growing financial inclusion sector, aligning with broader industry trends toward more inclusive financial services.

Bajaj Allianz logs general insurance premium of ₹1,324.67 Crore in Nov - Indiainfoline

Published Date: Tue, 10 Dec 2024

Bajaj Finserv Ltd reported strong premium performance from its unlisted insurance companies, Bajaj Allianz General Insurance and Bajaj Allianz Life Insurance, in November 2024. Bajaj Allianz General Insurance logged gross direct premiums of Rs 1 crore, bringing the total premium for the year to Rs 15 crore. Bajaj Allianz Life Insurance generated total new business premiums of Rs 6.5 crore in November, amounting to Rs 7 crore from April to November 2024. Bajaj Finserv's net profit increased by 8% to Rs 2,097 crore in the second quarter of FY25, while income from operations rose by 30% to Rs 33,704 crore. The company's general insurance division reported a profit after tax (PAT) of Rs 494 crore, up 6% from the previous year. The company attributed its success to expansion across its key operations while managing risk in several divisions.

Image not found.International Education Financing Spurs 15x Growth in Household Income - India Education Diary

Published Date: Tue, 10 Dec 2024

MPOWER Financing, a leading provider of no-cosigner loans and scholarships, has released its fourth annual Social Impact Report. The report highlights the transformative power of international education, with a focus on Indian students and the role of education financing in empowering them to succeed. The report showcases data from nearly 20,000 students across 182 countries, including significant representation from India. Key findings include 15x income growth for MPOWER students who graduate and find employment in the US, and 75% of students sending an average of $645 per month back home to support their families. The report also highlights the empowerment of women, with 68% of female MPOWER students pursuing STEM fields. The report concludes with a case study on the Indian diaspora, highlighting how international exposure fuels local entrepreneurship and economic development.
