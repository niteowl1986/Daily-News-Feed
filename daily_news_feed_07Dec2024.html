IDFC FIRST Bank has launched an AI-powered holographic digital avatar of Amitabh Bachchan, setting a new benchmark in customer engagement and digital innovation. The Holographic Extended Reality (HXR) device allows users to interact directly with the digital avatar and provides information on bank products and services. IDFC FIRST Bank's head of business excellence and corporate strategy, Shreepad Shende, said the bank is proud to be the first in the banking industry to bring this innovation to life. The bank's commitment to its digital-first philosophy and innovation-led customer-centric approach has led to this revolutionary technology.

Published Date: Fri, 06 Dec 2024

The 52nd MPC meeting took place from December 4 to 6, announcing decisions that will impact institutions and liquidity. The cash reserve ratio (CRR) was reduced by 25 basis points, with two installments from December 14 to 28. The CRR aims to manage liquidity, ensure bank stability, and control inflation. RBI Governor Das stated that the CRR cut will add Rs 1 trillion to the banking system, demonstrating a neutral policy stance. Experts welcomed the move, with Manappuram Finance's MD & CEO, V P Nandakumar, saying it will support the banking sector and economy. Ugro Capital's CFO, Kishor Lodha, noted that the move is positive, but controlling inflation remains crucial for further monetary easing.

CredAble, a global working capital tech platform, has partnered with PowerCred, a digital lending innovator, to drive innovation in working capital financing technology for banks in Indonesia.
