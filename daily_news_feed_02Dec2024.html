DBS India Expands Wealth Management Services for HNIs and NRIs - Hubbis

Published Date: Sun, 01 Dec 2024

DBS India has launched an enhanced wealth management offering, providing streamlined access to cross-border treasury services in key Asian markets. The service is tailored for high-net-worth individuals and non-resident Indians, with a digital account opening process for NRIs that can be completed within an hour. The bank's premium service, DBS Treasures, offers wealth management solutions, dedicated relationship managers, and exclusive privileges to clients with a total relationship value exceeding Rs 30 lakh. With over three decades of experience in India, DBS continues to strengthen its regional presence, recognizing the growing decentralization of wealth creation across the country.
