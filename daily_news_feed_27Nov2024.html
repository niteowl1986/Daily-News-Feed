Image not found.DBS Foundation Deploys SGD 88 Million to Widen Financial Inclusion and Provide Essential Needs to Vulnerable Communities Across Asia - ANI News

Published Date: Tue, 26 Nov 2024

The DBS Foundation has announced a SGD 88 million initiative to support vulnerable communities in Asia, focusing on improving living standards and enabling better access to essential needs. The funding will be used to launch 15 new initiatives, including those that focus on healthcare, education, and food security. The foundation aims to uplift the lives and livelihoods of two million low-income and underprivileged individuals by 2027. The initiative is part of the foundation's efforts to tackle ageing, with a focus on improving intergenerational connections, building accessible communal spaces, and encouraging employers to adopt a more inclusive workforce. The foundation has also launched a new Impact Beyond Award, offering prize money and mentorship to support businesses with innovative solutions that improve living standards and enable people to age with dignity. The foundation has supported 10 social enterprises, including two from India, which have positively transformed vulnerable communities through their innovative solutions.

Image not found.South Indian Bank Expands Operations with Inauguration of Mega Currency Chest in Kakkanad - Business News This Week

Published Date: Wed, 27 Nov 2024

South Indian Bank has opened its 2nd Mega Currency Chest at Kakkanad, Ernakulam, its 6th currency chest. The facility was inaugurated by Mr. Thomas Mathew, Regional Director of the Reserve Bank of India, in the presence of key officials from the Bank. The new currency chest will cater to the Bank's branches in Ernakulam and adjacent regions, streamlining cash management and enhancing operational efficiency. Mr. Thomas Mathew emphasized the partnership between the Reserve Bank of India and South Indian Bank in serving the public. Mr. P R Seshadri, Managing Director & CEO of South Indian Bank, said the inauguration marks a key milestone in the Bank's efforts to enhance efficiency and reliability of its services. With this new addition, South Indian Bank strengthens its infrastructure and reinforces its position as a leading financial institution committed to meeting the changing needs of its customers.

Image not found.Bajaj Finserv Health to Invest INR 1,000 Crore in Tech and Services, Launches 'Health Saathi' Concierge - Elets

Published Date: Tue, 26 Nov 2024

**Bajaj Finserv Health Invests INR 1,000 Crore to Enhance Healthcare Services** Bajaj Finserv Health, a subsidiary of Bajaj Finserv, is investing INR 1,000 crore to advance its products and technology. The company has acquired Vidal Health and plans to introduce innovative services, including the Health Saathi concierge service. This service aims to address challenges in healthcare delivery, such as paper-based systems and limited customer awareness of TPA rights. The company is also introducing value-based care products, such as a maternity care plan that offers holistic support. Bajaj Finserv Health has a robust network of over 100,000 doctors and 16,000 hospitals, handling 150,000 monthly transactions. The company's initiatives align with the National Digital Health Mission, emphasizing cashless and accessible healthcare for all.
