Image not found.The BNPL Credit Score Revolution: Navigating Risks and Rewards in Fintech - AInvest

Published Date: Sat, 28 Jun 2025

The integration of Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL) data into credit scoring models starting in 2025 will significantly impact the financial sector. FICO's new credit scores will incorporate BNPL repayment history, providing a more comprehensive view of consumer creditworthiness. This shift presents opportunities for fintech companies specializing in credit analytics and risk assessment, as they can leverage AI to quantify previously unreported debt and assist lenders in managing risk. However, the increased transparency also poses challenges for BNPL providers and consumers, as missed payments will be reflected in credit scores, potentially leading to higher default rates and stricter lending practices. Investors should carefully evaluate BNPL companies' strategies for aligning with the new credit scoring landscape and consider diversifying into credit analytics and established payment giants. The inclusion of BNPL data in credit scores marks a crucial step towards a more transparent and accountable financial ecosystem.

Image not found.DBS Bank India waives prepayment and foreclosure charges for registered Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises - Adgully.com

Published Date: Sat, 28 Jun 2025

DBS Bank India has eliminated prepayment and foreclosure charges for all UDYAM-registered Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs). This move, effective immediately, applies to both new and existing borrowers, aiming to enhance financial flexibility and align with regulatory transparency guidelines. The waiver addresses a common challenge faced by MSMEs, where foreclosure charges often discouraged early repayment or refinancing, hindering cash flow management. Sudarshan Chari, Managing Director and Head of SME Banking at DBS Bank India, emphasized the importance of flexible credit access for MSMEs, stating that the waiver enables businesses to optimize their financial resources. DBS Bank India remains committed to supporting MSMEs through its comprehensive SME banking proposition, which includes online account opening, hassle-free business loans, and innovative solutions like DBS IDEAL, a mobile-enabled corporate banking platform. The bank's efforts aim to empower enterprises across all stages of growth and strengthen their resilience in the market.

Image not found.Bob's small but mighty berry business - NZ Herald

Published Date: Sat, 28 Jun 2025

Robert Teal, a Cambridge orchard owner, has achieved success with a thriving berry business on a relatively small plot of land. Teal, a finalist in the 2025 Ballance Farm Environment Awards, won the Rabobank AgriBusiness Management Award and the New Zealand Farm Environment Trust Innovation Award. He transitioned from dairy farming to horticulture, strategically diversifying his income streams while waiting for plantings to mature. Teal's innovative approach includes utilizing poly tunnels for early berry production, adapting a golf cart for automated spraying, and repurposing pushchairs into berry collection trolleys. His commitment to sustainable practices is evident in his meticulous weed and pest management strategies and efficient water usage. Teal's success story exemplifies the potential for profitability and innovation in smaller-scale horticultural operations.
