Inside DBS Foundation's Next Decade of "Doing Well by Doing Good" - Forbes India

Published Date: Mon, 25 Nov 2024

DBS Bank has a renewed strategy to support social entrepreneurs and vulnerable communities, committing to invest up to S$1 billion over the next 10 years. The DBS Foundation hosted the inaugural Impact Beyond Summit on ageing societies, marking 10 years of its existence. The Foundation aims to find and support solutions that address the challenges faced by ageing societies, including financial, social, and mental health issues. India is one of the six key regions for the Foundation, which has already supported around 40 social enterprises in the country through grants, accelerator partnerships, and mentoring. Two Indian social enterprises, Haqdarshak and S4S Technologies, were honored at the Summit for their significant impact and scale. The Foundation plans to focus on providing essential needs and fostering inclusion in its next decade of operations in India, with a focus on areas such as food and nutrition, education, and healthcare.

Eastspring Indonesia and Bank DBS Indonesia Launch the Eastspring ESGQ45 IDX KEHATI Index Mutual Fund: An ESG-Based Investment Solution -Asian Wealth Management and Asian Private Banking - Hubbis

Published Date: Mon, 25 Nov 2024

Eastspring Investments Indonesia and Bank DBS Indonesia have launched the Eastspring ESGQ45 IDX KEHATI Index Mutual Fund, a sustainable investment solution that emphasizes Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles. The fund offers a promising option for individuals seeking long-term investment products that contribute positively to the environment and society. The Eastspring ESGQ45 IDX KEHATI Index Mutual Fund is the first mutual fund to utilize the ESGQ45 IDX KEHATI index, which includes stocks of companies that are leaders in ESG ratings. The fund allocates 80-100% of its investments to equity instruments listed on the Indonesia Stock Exchange and included in the ESG Quality 45 IDX KEHATI Index. Bank DBS Indonesia, as the custodian bank, offers core custodian services such as securities transaction settlement, safekeeping, and corporate action processing. The launch of this product reflects the commitment of Bank DBS Indonesia and Eastspring Indonesia to play a key role in financial planning that emphasizes ESG principles.

Fraud Detection Software Market to Witness Massive Growth | Major Giants IBM, Dell Technologies, Oracle - Newstrail

Published Date: Mon, 25 Nov 2024

HTF MI recently released a 143-page report on the global fraud detection software market, which is expected to reach USD 60 billion by 2030. The market is segmented by types, applications, and geography, with key players including SAS Institute, NICE Actimize, and FICO. The report highlights the growing demand for fraud detection software across sectors like BFSI, retail, and insurance, driven by the increasing digitization of financial services and e-commerce. The market is also expected to be driven by the use of biometrics, AI-driven fraud prevention, and integration with blockchain technology. However, the report also notes that high implementation costs, data privacy concerns, and false positives in detection are major challenges facing the market. The report provides an in-depth analysis of the market, including its segments, applications, and geographic regions, as well as key trends, drivers, and challenges.
