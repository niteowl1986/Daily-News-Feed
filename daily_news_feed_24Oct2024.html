FICO Partners with TCS to Deliver Businesses Major Efficiency Gains through Analytics and Decisioning - businesswire.com

Published Date: Wed, 23 Oct 2024

FICO, a global analytics software leader, has announced a strategic partnership with Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) to bring advanced optimization and decision management solutions to businesses worldwide. TCS will use FICO's Platform to create industry-specific solutions powered by real-time, fully interpretable analytics, machine learning, and AI models. The partnership aims to help businesses in various industries, such as fleet management and supply chain optimization, harness the power of advanced analytics and decision technology. One of the initial areas of focus is truck roll optimization, which can significantly reduce costs, improve response times, and lower carbon emissions. TCS and FICO are also developing solutions for the telecommunications and media sectors, including a solution to reduce churn, increase revenue, and optimize cost for service providers. The partnership aims to help businesses make smarter, faster decisions and mitigate the risks associated with climate change.
