DBS Bank Opens Branch in Coimbatore Offering Services for HNIs, SMEs - SMEStreet

Published Date: Thu, 13 Feb 2025

DBS Bank India has opened its sixteenth branch in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, focusing on serving high-net-worth individuals, SMEs, and large corporations. The new flagship branch offers a comprehensive suite of products and services, including trade and FX solutions, working capital facilities, and consumer loans against property. DBS Treasures, the bank's proposition for NRI and private banking clients, has been enhanced with bespoke benefits and cross-border solutions. Recognizing Coimbatore's growing retiree population, DBS offers its DBS Golden Circle proposition, tailored to meet the specific needs of older citizens with features like higher deposit rates and priority services. Bharath Mani, Managing Director & Head National Distribution, DBS Bank India, highlighted Coimbatore's strategic importance due to its diverse customer base and growing digital penetration. DBS Bank India, consistently ranked as the 'Safest Bank in Asia,' aims to be the bank of choice for a wider range of clients in Coimbatore.

Image not found.PNB to Host MSME Outreach Programme Across 200 Locations on February 13 - Entrepreneur

Published Date: Thu, 13 Feb 2025

Punjab National Bank (PNB) is hosting its MSME Outreach Programme on February 13, 2025, across 200 locations in India. This initiative aims to provide tailored financial solutions, expert guidance, and easy loan access to support the growth of Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs). The event will feature customized credit solutions, competitive interest rates, digital banking tools, and government-backed schemes. Attendees can benefit from on-the-spot loan approvals, personalized consultations, and in-principle approvals for cash flow-based schemes. PNB has also introduced a digital tab for instant access to Digi MSME loans, streamlining the financing process. This program serves as a one-stop platform for entrepreneurs to secure funding, gain financial insights, and network with key stakeholders.
