Swiss PE eyes IT, pharma in India portfolio rejig after 300% gain - The Edge Malaysia

Published Date: Tue, 24 Dec 2024

Swiss private equity firm Partners Group Holding AG is reviewing its India portfolio, which has grown to over $8 billion since it started investing in the country a decade ago. The firm is looking to exit some investments and deploy the proceeds in sectors such as healthcare, technology, education, and shadow lending. Partners Group recently sold its 23% stake in retail chain Vishal Mega Mart Ltd for over $3 billion and is awaiting regulatory approvals to sell its stake in mortgage lender Aavas Financiers Ltd to CVC Capital Partners. The company is also planning an initial public offering for logistics solutions company Ecom Express Ltd, in which it owns a 40% stake. Partners Group expects to close the Aavas exit by the first half of 2025 and is looking to sell shares worth ₹14 billion in the Ecom Express IPO. The firm's partner and global head of private equity goods and products, Manas Tandon, believes there are attractive investment opportunities in India, particularly in healthcare, technology, and education.

Image not found.Aditya Birla Capital goes live on all three core financial services on ONDC - BusinessLine

Published Date: Mon, 23 Dec 2024

Aditya Birla Capital, a nonbanking finance company, has launched its financial services on the Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC) platform. This platform allows various buyer apps to offer Aditya Birla Capital's personal loans, health insurance, and mutual funds to customers without the need to download multiple apps. Aditya Birla Finance was an early adopter of the credit integration pilot phase, while Aditya Birla Health Insurance and Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC are among the first participants in integrating health insurance and mutual fund investments on the network. The ONDC network consists of over 200 apps, including buyer and seller apps. This integration aims to provide customers with a seamless and convenient experience. By partnering with ONDC, Aditya Birla Capital is expanding its reach and offering its financial services to a wider audience.

Image not found.Aditya Birla Capital becomes first BFSI firm to offer full suite of financial services on ONDC platform - The Financial Express

Published Date: Mon, 23 Dec 2024

Aditya Birla Capital has become the first BFSI company to offer lending, insurance, and mutual fund investments on the Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC) platform. This move aims to provide broader access to financial products, particularly in underserved markets, and support financial inclusion across the country. The integration with ONDC will enable consumers to access Aditya Birla Capital's financial services through various live Buyer Apps, streamlining their financial experiences. Aditya Birla Capital was an early adopter of ONDC's credit integration and has played a key role in shaping the overall ONDC ecosystem for financial services. T. Koshy, MD & CEO, ONDC, commented that the integration will help deepen financial inclusion in India. This partnership is a significant milestone in Aditya Birla Capital's commitment to simplifying finance and making its offerings accessible to every Indian.
