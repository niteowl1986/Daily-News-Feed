Why TF Bank has Selected NICE Actimize for AML Compliance - FinTech Magazine

Published Date: Fri, 29 Nov 2024

Financial institutions are under pressure to strengthen their compliance controls due to increased scrutiny from regulators. The total cost of financial crime compliance in EMEA reached $85 billion annually in 2024, with cryptocurrencies and digital payments being used for illicit activities. To reduce costs and improve efficiency, financial institutions are deploying automated solutions to manage compliance requirements. NICE Actimize, a financial crime prevention software provider, has secured a contract with TF Bank to implement its cloud-based antimoney laundering (AML) system across its European operations. The system uses AI to analyze customer behavior and flag suspicious patterns, and includes transaction monitoring software and customer due diligence tools. The implementation aims to reduce manual workload for compliance teams and improve the accuracy of suspicious activity detection.
