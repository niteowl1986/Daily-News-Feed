Published Date: Sun, 27 Oct 2024

IDFC First Bank reported a 15% year-on-year drop in net profit for Q2 FY25, at ₹1,434 crore. Net Interest Income (NII) rose 23% YoY to ₹4,788 crore, driven by growth in interest from lending activities. Operating income grew 21% YoY, while operating expenses rose 18% YoY. IDFC First Bank Reports 32% YOY Growth in Customer Deposits - SMEStreet

Published Date: Sun, 27 Oct 2024

IDFC FIRST Bank's Board of Directors approved unaudited financial results for Q2FY25, showing a 32% YOY growth in customer deposits to Rs. 2,18,026 crore. Retail deposits grew by 37% YOY to Rs. 1,75,300 crore, while CASA deposits increased by 37% YOY to Rs. 1,09,292 crore. The bank's cost of funds was 7.34% in Q2FY25, with a CASA ratio of 49.5%. Loans and advances increased by 22% YOY to Rs. 2,22,613 crore, with the retail book growing by 25% YoY. Gross NPA was 3.14% as of September 30, 2024, while net NPA was 1.23%. The bank's core operating profit grew by 28% YOY to Rs. 1,857 crore, excluding trading gains.

Emkay Global has maintained a "Buy" recommendation for UGRO Capital with a target price of Rs 360, representing a potential upside of 50% from its current price. The company's strong AUM growth, focused expansion in the microenterprise segment, and operational efficiency are expected to drive its growth. Emkay Global anticipates a 50% upside potential in UGRO Capital's stock price, based on projected improvements in operating leverage and borrowing costs, as well as an expected RoA of 4% by FY27.
