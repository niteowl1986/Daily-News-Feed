Bajaj Finance secures $400 million loan from IFC to boost electric vehicle sales - Telematics Wire

Published Date: Fri, 08 Nov 2024

The International Finance Corporation (IFC) has partnered with Bajaj Finance to provide a $400 million loan to support women-owned microenterprises and increase access to climate finance for electric vehicles and energy-efficient consumer goods. The investment aims to help India meet its climate goals, promote financial inclusion, and boost competition in the climate finance industry. The loan will also increase financing availability for consumers purchasing electric vehicles and energy-efficient consumer goods. Additionally, the partnership will enhance Bajaj Finance's capacity to finance and assist female microborrowers and women-owned microenterprises. India's energy sector is expanding rapidly, leading to a significant increase in greenhouse gas emissions. The IFC has allocated a record $56 billion to private businesses and financial institutions in developing nations in fiscal 2024, with operations in over 100 countries.

INFOGRAPHIC: Top LatAm Startup Funding — Week 45 - Techloy

Published Date: Fri, 08 Nov 2024

Fairplay, a Mexican fintech, has secured a $35 million credit line from BBVA Spark. This funding will enable Fairplay to expand its services to new industries, enhance its financial products, and scale its operations across Mexico. Founded in 2019, Fairplay provides working capital solutions to small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in Mexico, improving cash flow and supporting growth. The company's innovative financial products aim to empower SMEs to access the working capital they need to grow and contribute to the Mexican economy. With its new funding, Fairplay will invest in improving its technology platform to offer more seamless and flexible financing solutions. This deal follows BBVA's broader efforts to invest in fast-growing, innovative companies in emerging markets.
