Image not found.PSBs split over perquisite tax liability for staff - The Financial Express

Published Date: Mon, 16 Dec 2024

Public sector bank employees in India are facing a retrospective tax on perquisites, which will be paid this financial year. While State Bank of India and Bank of Baroda will cover the tax liability for their employees, Punjab National Bank and Union Bank of India are asking employees to bear the cost. To ease the financial strain, Punjab National Bank is offering personal loans to employees, but they will have to pay perquisite tax on the loan as well. Bank employees are unhappy with this arrangement, with some calling for the bank to pay the tax on their behalf. The total tax outgo for Punjab National Bank is estimated to be Rs 400-500 crore. The tax deductions will start next month, with employees required to pay tax retrospectively from April this year.
