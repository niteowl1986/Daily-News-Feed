Image not found.DBS Bank India opens new flagship branch in Coimbatore catering to the affluent segment - Passionate In Marketing

Published Date: Fri, 14 Feb 2025

DBS Bank India inaugurated its newest flagship branch in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, expanding its presence in a key market. The branch will serve a diverse clientele, including high-net-worth individuals, SMEs, large corporations, NRIs, and retirees. DBS Treasures will cater to premium clients with enhanced benefits and cross-border solutions, while DBS Golden Circle offers tailored services for senior citizens, such as higher deposit rates and healthcare benefits. Recognizing Coimbatore's thriving real estate market, the branch will provide Consumer Loan Against Property (CLAP) to meet the growing demand for financing. The bank aims to empower customers with a seamless experience through its phygital network of 16 branches and digital solutions, enabling them to "Live more, Bank less." DBS Bank India's commitment to safety and innovation is reflected in its consistent recognition as the "Safest Bank in Asia" and the launch of its super premium credit card, DBS Vantage.

Image not found.UGRO Capital Reinforces Global Impact Alliance With FMO, Securing ~INR 2,600 Million In NCDs - APN News

Published Date: Fri, 14 Feb 2025

UGRO Capital, a leading Indian DataTech NBFC focused on MSME lending, has secured INR 2,600 million in financing from FMO, the Dutch entrepreneurial development bank. This investment will support initiatives aimed at women empowerment, addressing regional inequalities, and fostering youth empowerment. The NCD issuance was fully subscribed by FMO, demonstrating their commitment to inclusive and sustainable prosperity through entrepreneurship. This collaboration marks a repeat engagement for UGRO Capital and FMO, following a previous USD 30 million investment in December 2023. UGRO Capital has a proven track record of supporting small businesses, having extended credit to over 100,000 businesses in the past six years. This diversified funding approach not only strengthens UGRO Capital's financial position but also enables them to generate tangible social and economic impact.
