PNB Housing Finance, India's third-largest housing finance company, launched Roshni, a mascot symbolizing hope, positivity, and empowerment. Roshni, meaning "light" in Hindi, reflects the company's promise to illuminate the path to affordable housing for aspiring homeowners. A comprehensive 360-degree marketing campaign was rolled out across various media platforms, including television, print, outdoor, cinema, digital, and social media. The campaign aims to create a strong and consistent presence, engaging audiences across urban and semi-urban regions, and delivering messaging in multiple regional languages. Roshni, a 10-year-old girl with a sun for a head, personifies the company's promise and serves as a relatable figure, simplifying the journey towards affordable housing. The campaign aims to achieve Rs 5,000 crore in affordable housing loan book through 200 dedicated Roshni branches nationwide by the end of FY25.

Image not found.Dutch economy to grow in 2025, although with major regional differences - IO+

Published Date: Thu, 19 Dec 2024

According to a report by Rabobank, all Dutch regions are expected to experience economic growth in 2025, with no forecasted shrinkage. The report projects a 1% increase in the Dutch economy overall, with Greater Amsterdam and Brainport Eindhoven leading the growth with 2% and 1.5% rates, respectively. The service sector, particularly information and communication, is expected to grow robustly at 4%, while the construction sector will bounce back after a contraction year. The trade sector is also expected to grow by 1.5% due to a surge in consumption and exports. Rabobank's analysis also highlights that certain regions, such as South East Friesland, South East South Holland, and South West Drente, are expected to outperform the national average. The report also notes that the potential impact of US import tariffs could affect regions such as North Brabant, Zeeland, Limburg, and Overijssel, which are major exporters to the US.
