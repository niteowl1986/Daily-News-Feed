Image not found.FICO to Factor “Buy Now, Pay Later” Loans Into Credit Scores - The Military Wallet

Published Date: Wed, 02 Jul 2025

FICO, a leading credit scoring provider, is launching two new credit score tools, FICO Score 10 BNPL and FICO Score 10 T BNPL, which will incorporate data from Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL) loans. These new scores aim to provide a more comprehensive view of a consumer's creditworthiness by factoring in BNPL payment history. The move comes as BNPL usage has surged, particularly among younger consumers and those with limited credit history, often used to manage rising costs. FICO's research suggests that consumers with responsible BNPL payment habits may see their scores increase or remain stable under the new model. However, late or missed BNPL payments could negatively impact credit scores, similar to other forms of credit. While the new scores will be available in the fall, widespread adoption may take time as not all BNPL companies share data with credit bureaus and lenders may not opt to use the new tools.
