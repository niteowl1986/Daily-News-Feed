Average U.S. FICO Score Holds at 717 Despite Economic Uncertainty - Business Wire

Published Date: Wed, 09 Oct 2024

The national average US FICO Score has decreased by one point to 717, according to new analysis from FICO. This is the same as when FICO last reported on it earlier this year. FICO Scores are used by 90% of top US lenders to assess consumer credit risk. The scores are dynamic and evolve as shifts in borrower behavior are reflected in the data maintained by the three primary US consumer reporting agencies. FICO consistently releases the average US FICO Score, which serves as an indicator of credit health for consumers nationwide. The company's latest analysis shows that high interest rates and consumer prices are adding pressure to household finances, resulting in continued increases in missed borrower payments and consumer debt levels.

Public companies account for 28% of PNB Housing Finance Limited's (NSE:PNBHOUSING) ownership, while individual investors account for 24% - Simply Wall St

Published Date: Thu, 10 Oct 2024

PNB Housing Finance Limited is a housing finance company in India, and its share registry shows that public companies hold the most number of shares, around 28%. Individual investors account for 24% of the company's stockholders. The company has institutional investors, including Punjab National Bank, which owns 28% of the shares. The top 3 shareholders collectively control more than half of the company's shares, giving them considerable power to influence the company's decisions. The general public, mostly comprising individual investors, has a 24% ownership stake, while private equity firms own 20%. It's essential to consider the different ownership groups, but also important to look at risks and the company's future prospects, which can be influenced by analyst recommendations and company performance.
