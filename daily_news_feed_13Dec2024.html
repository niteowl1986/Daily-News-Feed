UGRO Capital receives a patent for its credit scoring model - Bizz Buzz

Published Date: Thu, 12 Dec 2024

UGRO Capital, a leading DataTech NBFC, has achieved a milestone by receiving a patent for its proprietary credit scoring model, GRO Score. This model evaluates creditworthiness using alternate data, such as repayment histories, banking transactions, and GST records, without formal income documentation. GRO Score has assessed over 1 lakh loan applications, analyzing over 1 lakh bureau records, 1 lakh bank statements, and 80,000 GST reports, unlocking vital capital for MSMEs across India. The patent underscores UGRO Capital's commitment to transforming MSME lending and its mission to bridge the MSME credit gap. The company's founder, Shachindra Nath, emphasized that the patent reaffirms their commitment to driving innovation in credit evaluation and serving last-mile MSMEs. The GRO Score patent is a significant achievement for UGRO Capital, demonstrating its dedication to technological excellence and its role in shaping a sustainable and inclusive financial ecosystem.
