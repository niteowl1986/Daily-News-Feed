Published Date: Mon, 28 Oct 2024

FICO, a global analytics software leader, has announced a new analytic tool called the FICO Score Mortgage Simulator. This innovative tool simulates the potential impacts to a consumer's FICO Score with various changes to their credit report data, such as reducing credit card balances or deleting a collection account. Mortgage professionals can use this tool to help potential borrowers gauge how different credit decisions could affect their FICO Score and open up more loan options and favorable interest rates. The FICO Score Mortgage Simulator is the only score simulator for mortgage professionals that uses FICO Scores and actual FICO Score algorithms. With this tool, lenders can run credit event scenarios from an applicant's credit report data and determine the potential impact to their actual FICO Score. The FICO Score Mortgage Simulator aims to responsibly expand credit access to more people and help them achieve the dream of homeownership.

Aavas Financiers Acquisition, CCI allow to Aquilo House Pte. Ltd. - The Samikhsya

Published Date: Tue, 29 Oct 2024

The Competition Commission of India has approved the acquisition of Aavas Financiers Limited by Aquilo House Pte. Ltd. Aavas Financiers Limited is a newly incorporated entity owned by Relevant CVC Funds, managed by CVC Capital Partners plc. The target, Aavas Financiers Limited, is a non-deposit taking housing finance company registered with the National Housing Bank. Its business activities include providing home loans, MSME business loans, and loan against property, as well as distributing insurance products. The proposed transaction involves the acquisition of shares and control by Aquilo House Pte. Ltd. through share sale agreements and a mandatory open offer in accordance with the Securities and Exchange Board of India regulations. The acquisition is subject to the approval of the Securities and Exchange Board of India and other regulatory authorities.
